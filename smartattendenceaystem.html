How It Works

A simple 3-step process powered by modern tech.

  1. 1. Identify: Use your unique identity (Face, Fingerprint, ID Card/QR Code).
  2. 2. Verify: The system instantly matches your identity to its records.
  3. 3. Record: Your attendance is logged with the exact time. Done in seconds!

Key Benefits

Why it's better for everyone.

  • Saves Time: Frees up time for more important activities.
  • Stops Fake Attendance: Ensures only the right person is marked present.
  • Highly Accurate: Provides error-free data for payroll or academic records.
  • Easy Reporting: Generate any attendance report with a single click.

Who Uses It?

Smart attendance is useful everywhere.

  • Schools & Colleges: To track student presence and improve punctuality.
  • Offices: To manage employee work hours and automate payroll.
  • Hospitals & Factories: To manage shifts for a large number of staff efficiently.
